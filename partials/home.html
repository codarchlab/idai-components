Usage

Just copy the file to your project and link the source idai-components-[version].min.js from your project's index.html. 

<script src="dest/idai-components-0.9.0.min.js"></script>

Do this after the angular link and before the links to your application scripts, which make use of the library.

Include these dependencies from within your main module: 

angular.module('sampleApp',[
	'idai.components',
	'idai.templates'
]);

See the pages linked in the top menu for detailed documentation of the different components.
